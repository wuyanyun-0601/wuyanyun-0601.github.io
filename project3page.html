NAUTILUS 2035
海洋载具

NAUTILUS 2035依靠波动的长鳍产生推力行进，减少对周边生物的干扰。通过圆环结构可以与海洋生物进行互动，并在座舱内得到它们的反馈。相信它能够为未来海下居民带来奇妙的海底漫游体验。

With the unique dynamic structure, NAUTILUS 2035 will rely on the undulating long fins to generate thrust in order to minimize the interference toward marine life .People can interact with marine life with the help of an annular information system and get feedback from them through the audio and video playing device.

未来的海洋生态系统中，终极的浪漫是运用科技实现人与自然的共存与融合。

In the future marine ecosystem,the ultimate romance is to use science and technology to realize the coexistence and integration of human and nature.
