RE-spire
呼吸训练器

围手术期呼吸训练对需手术患者的康复有很大帮助，但因高费用、内容枯燥和过程缺乏科学记录而常被忽视。RE-spire通过不同吹嘴和可拆换气道能参与整个康复流程且能二次流通。其界面记录了康复期的数据进行比较，以游戏式的板块增加患者积极性。

Peri-operative breath trainings are helpful to surgery needed patients, but they are often ignored due to costliness, dullness and lack of data tracking. With different mouthpieces and an alternate airway, Re-spire can be used in the whole recovery process and then enter the secondary market. The interface can show and compare the data during the process, and there are games to increase patients' gumption.

我们应当如何帮助手术病人进行科学、有效且经济的呼吸功能康复训练，使他们的身体机能得到更好的恢复？

How can we help patients after surgury carry out scientific, effective and economical respiratory rehabilitation training, so as to better recover their physical function?
